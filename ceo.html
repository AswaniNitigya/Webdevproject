Meet Our Founder & CEO

"Visionary leadership is the cornerstone of building lasting success."

Nitigya Aswani, the founder and CEO of Aswani Group, is a pioneer in innovation and entrepreneurship. With over 20 years of experience in diversified industries, Nitigya has been instrumental in shaping the company's vision and driving its growth.

Under his leadership, Aswani Group has expanded its footprint across sectors like automotive, technology, and real estate, establishing a reputation for excellence and trust. A firm believer in sustainable development, Nitigya is committed to empowering communities and creating value for stakeholders.

Nitigya holds an MBA from a leading business school and has been recognized globally for his contributions to industry and innovation. In his free time, he enjoys mentoring young entrepreneurs and exploring new technologies.
